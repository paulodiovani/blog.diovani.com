The timeline of AI in software development has shifted from simple code completion to autonomous agents capable of managing entire codebases and workflows. Since the public debut of ChatGPT, the industry has transitioned from "Copilots" (human-led assistants) to "Agents" (AI-led execution). [1, 2, 3, 4, 5]

## Key Methodologies & Paradigm Shifts

- Vibe Coding (2025–2026): A shift toward high-level natural language descriptions where the developer "vibe checks" the agent's output rather than writing lines of code.
- Orchestration over Coding: Gartner predicts that by late 2026, 90% of engineers will shift from manual coding to orchestrating autonomous agents.
- Long-Horizon Reasoning: Modern agents like Claude Opus 4.6 and GPT-5 (introduced late 2025) focus on "deep reasoning," allowing them to plan and execute projects over hours rather than seconds. [15, 17, 18, 19, 21]
